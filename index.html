Rachael Yamagata

Click to know more about her

Rachael Yamagata is an American singer-songwriter and pianist from Arlington, Virginia. I've been her live in Chengdu on 12 September,2017. And the night was soooo brilliant, I was extremely impressed by the atmosphere she created, the music and strangers around me. After that night, the idea that making a website for her occurs to me, because I want more people to know such a great singer. So, relax and enjoy her songs.
